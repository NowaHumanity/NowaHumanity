🚀 Roadmap - The Future of NoWa Humanity

We are actively developing NoWa Humanity. Below is our structured vision for the next phases of growth and expansion.

🌍 Phase 1: Establishing the Foundation (2025-2026)

  • ✅ Launch official website and establish initial online presence
  • ✅ Publish the NoWa Humanity Manifesto and Vision Statement
  • ✅ Create official community spaces (Discord, Telegram, Reddit, GitHub)
  • 🔄 Expand outreach and attract first-wave contributors
  • 🔄 Develop primary governance framework for community decision-making

🚀 Phase 2: Growth and Community Development (2026-2028)

  • ⏳ Develop and launch decentralized collaboration tools
  • ⏳ Establish an open-source educational platform (NoWa Learning)
  • ⏳ Build core development teams for technological advancements
  • ⏳ Initiate global awareness and engagement campaigns
  • ⏳ Begin testing decentralized voting and governance systems

🌌 Phase 3: Global Influence and Implementation (2028-2031)

  • ⏳ Deploy NoWa Coin – a decentralized, purpose-driven digital economy
  • ⏳ Expand global participation through partnerships with ethical organizations
  • ⏳ Establish pilot programs for decentralized city governance models
  • ⏳ Host international NoWa Humanity Summits
  • ⏳ Strengthen cross-industry collaborations for sustainable technological progress

🚀 Phase 4: Future Civilization and Expansion (2031-2035 and Beyond)

  • ⏳ Establish autonomous communities based on NoWa Humanity principles
  • ⏳ Introduce post-nation governance structures focusing on decentralization
  • ⏳ Integrate advanced AI and automation to eliminate forced labor economies
  • ⏳ Expand research into sustainable interplanetary colonization
  • ⏳ Launch first steps toward becoming an interstellar civilization
